# Ren Zhengfei: Starting Huawei at 43

**Modern Era · 1944-Present** | Founder of Huawei, from midlife crisis to Fortune Global 500

---

## Midlife Crisis: Everything Reset at 43

In 1987, at age 43, Ren Zhengfei faced the biggest crisis of his life:

- **Lost his job** — Fired from a state-owned enterprise, no pension
- **Lost his family** — His wife divorced him and took the children
- **Crushing debt** — Owed 2 million yuan (an astronomical sum at the time)
- **Aging parents** — Still had to support two elderly parents

At 43, everything reset to zero. Many people would have given up at this point.

> *"But I still have two hands. I can still work."*  
> — Ren Zhengfei, recalling his mindset at the time

---

## Borrowing 20,000 to Start Huawei

Ren Zhengfei borrowed 21,000 yuan and founded Huawei in Shenzhen:

- **1987** — Reselling Hong Kong telephone switches, earning the margin
- **1990** — Decided to pursue independent R&D, no longer just a reseller
- **1993** — C&C08 switch succeeded. Huawei had its own product.
- **1996** — Attacked the rural market. "Surround the cities from the countryside."

The shift from reselling to independent R&D was the **critical turning point**.

> *"Don't be a reseller. Have your own technology.*  
> *Reselling can be cut off anytime. Technology is your lifeline."*

---

## Crisis Consciousness: Winter Always Comes

After Huawei's success, Ren Zhengfei didn't relax. He wrote *"Huawei's Winter"*:

> *"For the past ten years, every single day I've thought about failure. I ignore success.*  
> *No matter the circumstances, you must have crisis consciousness.*  
> *Winter always comes. Whoever is prepared will survive."*

This is not pessimism. This is **clarity**.

Because of this crisis consciousness, when America sanctioned Huawei, they had backup chips and a backup operating system ready.

---

## Core Technology: Never Depend on Others

Ren Zhengfei firmly believed: core technology must be in your own hands.

- **Chips** — HiSilicon semiconductor, developing Kirin chips
- **Operating System** — HarmonyOS, not dependent on Android
- **5G** — Ten years of investment, now a global 5G leader
- **Talent** — Recruiting "genius youth" globally, highest salary 2 million yuan

In 2019, America sanctioned Huawei. Ren Zhengfei said:

> *"We are already prepared.*  
> *Without American chips, Huawei will still survive."*
